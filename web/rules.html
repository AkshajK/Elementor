    The "player" is a lone atom, trapped in a world of free protons and electrons.

    As an atom, you must strive to reach the element at the end of the periodic table -- Ununoctium -- without going unstable. "Unstable" means to have a net charge of greater than 2, or less than -2.

    To reach your goal, you must obtain as many protons as you can, while still taking electrons to keep your net charge close to 0.

    Arrow keys are to move.
